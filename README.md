# Institute360

Institute360 is a modern, AI-powered Education CRM and Institute Management Platform designed for schools, colleges, universities, coaching centers, and training institutes.

Our mission is to simplify educational administration by providing a single platform to manage the complete student lifecycle—from lead generation and admissions to academics, finance, communication, and alumni engagement.

## Vision

To empower educational institutions with intelligent, scalable, and user-friendly technology that enhances learning, administration, and decision-making.

## Planned Modules

- Admissions CRM
- Lead Management
- Student Information System (SIS)
- Course & Batch Management
- Attendance Management
- Fees & Finance
- Learning Management (LMS)
- Faculty Management
- Parent & Student Portal
- Timetable & Scheduling
- Examination & Results
- Communication (Email, SMS, WhatsApp)
- AI Assistant
- Reports & Analytics
- Mobile App
- API Integration
